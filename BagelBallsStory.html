BagelBalls Inc. started back in 1908 as a family run business. My great-grandfather Piekarz Baacker was a German immigrant to Poland, where he started a bakery in order to provide for his children. Piekarz asked himself, "who really has time to eat a whole bagel?" And thus, BagelBalls Inc. was born!

Today, BagelBalls Inc. is run by his three great-grandchildren of the Baacker family.
